top 10 hairstyles of 2018

by Dabira Akinwumi Hair Styles 5 comments

2018 was a year of crazy and dynamic hairstyles. We saw a lot of classic hairstyles reinvigorated and also witness the birth of some new ones. Although it was very hard compiling this list, here are our top 10 hairstyles of 2018:

1. ATHE UPDATED SHAG: Coming in at number 1 is a 70s style with an update of a deep side part and fewer layers. Just part your hair on the side, then just let it air-dry — your natural wave will take care of the rest.

2. A ‘60S FLIP: At number 2 is the 60’s flip. To get the look, start with dry hair. Using a 1½ inch curling iron, curl just the ends of hair. Finish with a strong-hold hairspray, like that old-school favorite.

3. HALF-UP TOP KNOT: To achieve this look let hair air dry, then make a horizontal part from ear to ear. Twist hair up into a top knot, then secure with a ponytail holder.

4. THE SPIKY PIXIE: Use your fingers to work a quarter-size dollop of gel through (dry) hair, pulling small sections up and in different directions to create a spiky look.

5. SMOOTH AND SLEEK: Use a flat iron to get hair as straight as possible. (Spray hair with a heat protectant first to prevent damage.) Then apply gel from roots to ends, using a fine-tooth comb to distribute gel evenly through hair and let dry.

6. THE SIDE-PART LOW PONY: To get Freida Pinto's red-carpet-worthy pony, start by flat ironing hair. Then make a deep part on one side, gather hair into a pony at the nape of the neck, then secure with an elastic band.

7. HEADBANDS: The name is self-explanatory, this simple style will give you an edge.

8. LONG, LONG LOCKS: You'll probably need help from extensions to get the full length like Kim, but if you decide to grow your hair out instead, be sure to trim it every few months to keep your strands looking healthy.

9. TWO-TONED BLONDE: If you're changing up your color, keep the roots dark underneath and have your stylist blend out the top to a bright blonde or silvery grey.

10. BARELY-THERE WAVES: Just grab your flat iron and create a few subtle waves here and there by twisting it as you pull it through hair. It will take a few minutes, tops.

10. HONOURABLE MENTIONS: MIDI CUTS, FAUX PERMS, FAKE BANGS, HIGH AND TIGHT PONY.

GOING BALD: ITS ALL IN YOUR HEAD

by Dabira Akinwumi Life Style 2 comments

Going bald is something most men deal with at a certain age, some younger, some older. In all, in reality, going bald is nothing to be ashamed of. However, you do have a few options at your disposal for dealing with baldness.

Use Rogain: A doctor will relocate bald resistant hair follicles from the back of your head to the balding areas on the top of your head. Because the hair follicles on the back of your head are resistant to hair loss, in theory, you’ll never have to worry about balding again.
